The art of the
confident pause

Silence is not weakness — it is authority. Most people rush to fill silence out of anxiety. Leaders pause deliberately. A two-second pause before answering signals you are thinking, not scrambling. It creates gravity around your next words.

Today's practice phrase
"That's a great question. Let me think about that for a moment."

Why this works

When you pause, listeners lean in. Your brain also performs better — rushed speech bypasses deliberate thought. Measured replies signal confidence across all professional cultures.
